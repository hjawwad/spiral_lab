# Spiral Design System

## Brand
**Company:** Spiral Lab

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Teal Blue:** `#2B5F6F` (Background)
- **Lime Green:** `#7FD944` (Accent/Logo)

### Secondary Colors
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Text)
- **Light Teal:** `#4A8799` (Lighter variant for hover states)

## Logo
- Lightning bolt/spiral shape in lime green
- Clean, modern geometric design
- Works well on dark teal background

## Visual Style
- Modern, tech-forward  aesthetic
- Geometric shapes and patterns
- Lightning/spiral motifs for backgrounds
- High contrast (lime green on teal)
- Clean, minimal approach

## Brand Vibe
Energy, innovation, forward-thinking, technology
